    Our Projects

    Explore our impressive portfolio of past projects! Each venture showcases our dedication, creativity, and expertise in delivering exceptional results. From innovative startups to successful business transformations, our track record speaks for itself. Dive into our portfolio and discover the tangible impact we've made in various industries. Get inspired and envision the possibilities for your own business!

    Puzzology

    Embark on an enchanting journey with Puzzology, the brainchild of Wan! Our startup is revolutionizing education by offering an exciting product—a captivating puzzle designed to unravel the mysteries of Egyptian history for children. Say goodbye to dull lessons and hello to a world where learning is an adventure! Dive into the past, piece by piece, and watch as your child discovers the wonders of Egypt while having a blast.

    Meta pill

    Introducing Meta Pill, your electronic nurse transforming medication management! Combatting confusion and dosage errors, our upgraded pill organizer features 7x4 compartments with LED lights for precise alarms. Perfect for seniors, a touch screen displays medication details, putting control at your fingertips. Plus, our eco-friendly choice of polypropylene (PP) ensures a low carbon footprint and easy recyclability, reducing the environmental impact of medical waste. Join us in revolutionizing healthcare with Meta Pill!

    Trackify

    Introducing Trackify – the customizable chip revolutionizing item security! Connect it to your mobile, bracelet, wallet, and more. If your precious belongings stray beyond 5 meters, Trackify's Bluetooth alerts you instantly. The app takes charge, notifying you of the item's location, ensuring you never lose track. Your guardian against loss – customize, connect, and stay in control. Because losing track is a thing of the past with Trackify!

    X-made

    Unveiling our empowering app – a marketplace showcasing handmade treasures crafted by talented girls and women eager to enhance their financial standing. Explore a world of unique products, each telling a story of skill and determination. Support and uplift these artisans by discovering and owning their creations through our app, where every purchase makes a meaningful impact.

    Akl Betak

    Meet AklBetak – the game-changer addressing women's empowerment, job opportunities, and healthy eating! This vital app empowers women to showcase their culinary skills while promoting quality home-cooked meals over junk food. Customers simply place orders for their desired dish, and the chosen woman prepares it with care. A dedicated delivery person ensures the food reaches the customer, with a potential partnership with Marsol to enhance services. Customers can provide feedback by rating the food, making AklBetak a revolutionary force for positive change.

    SYL Blood

    Introducing SYL Blood Donation – an app tackling two critical issues: blood donation and financial barriers to healthcare. By forging partnerships with hospitals, users earn points for donating blood, stored in the app's wallet. This innovative approach motivates people to contribute and accumulate points. In times of need, users can redeem these points for discounted services and surgeries in affiliated hospitals, creating a sustainable cycle of blood donation and healthcare support. Join SYL Blood Donation and make a difference in saving lives while ensuring accessible healthcare for all.
